About

I was born in Mexico and raised in San Antonio, Texas. I am currently a student at Cornell University with a strong interest in sustainability and the re-use of materials in architecture. I've always had a deep love for art, especially the work of Mexican muralists-whose bold expression and cultural depth continue to inspire me. My experiences across places and disciplines shape the way I see and think about architecture today.
